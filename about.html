Open Mission Control Techologies for Mission Control Web Services (Open MCT for MCWS)

Open MCT for MCWS is a next-generation mission control framework for visualization of data on desktop and mobile devices. Open MCT for MCWS is built on the Open MCT Platform, developed at NASA Ames Research Center in Silicon Valley, in collaboration with NASA AMMOS and the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of Technology (under its contract with NASA, 80NM0018D0004).
